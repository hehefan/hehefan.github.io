BIO

I am currently a ZJU100 Young Professor with the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University. Before that, I was a Research Fellow with Prof. Mohan S. Kankanhalli at National University of Singapore. My research focuses on deep learning, computer vision, multimedia and AI for science. I received my PhD degree at University of Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. Yi Yang.

I am looking for highly motivated visiting students with exceptional coding ability and research experience. Please drop me an email if you're interested.
